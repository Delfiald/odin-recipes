Hello, I'm Dogeheck, an aspiring full-stack developer embarking on a journey of learning from scratch. I'm focused on mastering the foundational aspects of web development, including HTML, CSS, and basic JavaScript. My passion for coding drives me to explore the intricacies of front-end and back-end development, starting with building responsive and visually appealing web interfaces using HTML and CSS. I'm also diving into the world of server-side programming to understand how data is processed and managed on the web. As I navigate through this exciting path, I'm eager to share my progress, challenges, and insights with fellow learners and developers. Join me on this coding adventure, where every line of code brings me closer to achieving my goal of becoming a skilled full-stack developer.
